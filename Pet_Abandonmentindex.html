Animal Abandonment

Stop Animal Abandonment

There are 13,600 estimated animal shelters nationwide. 7.6 MILLION companion animals enter animal shelters each year.

Abandoned

Only 1 out of every 10 dogs born will find a permanent home.

Set "Free"

Most people think that setting their animals free is helping them. Most animals being set free can't fend for themselves and end up starving or being killed the first night.

Animals Have Feelings Too

2.7 million animals are euthanized each year.

Shelter Animals

Of the approximately 7.6 million companion animals who enter shelters nationwide every year, approximately 3.9 million are dogs and 3.4 million are cats.
